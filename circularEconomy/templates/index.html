What is Circular economy?

The Circular Economy refers to an economic model that is regenerative intentionally and by design, based on renewable energy sources, minimizes, detects and eliminates waste with careful planning. One of many definitions of Circular Economy is "An economy where the value of products, materials and resources is retained in the economy for as long as possible and waste production is minimized.

The importance of Circular Economy in Automobile industry.

The automotive industry consumes large quantities of raw materials, energy and water. It also produces large volumes of waste. It is necessary to create a universal monitoring framework that will be used as a guide for the adoption of circular economy models.

What is the purpose of this page?

The importance of Circular Economy cannot be doubted. So many companies, organizations and even countries tried to make up their own framework, which aims on monitoring the Circular Progress. There are frameworks with macro (general) estimation i.e. countries and others with micro (more specific) i.e. companies and even more. This page is a tool, which applies our monitoring framework ,on Automobile Industries, based on mostly the EU general Monitoring Framework.
