What is this Tool?

So many companies, organizations and even countries tried to make up their own framework, which aims on monitoring the Circular Progress. There are frameworks with macro (general) estimation i.e. countries and others with micro (more specific) i.e. companies and even more. This web application is a tool, which applies our monitoring framework ,on Automobile Industries, based on mostly the EU general Monitoring Framework. Our tool aims on rating and monitoring an Automobile Industry based on its circularity.
